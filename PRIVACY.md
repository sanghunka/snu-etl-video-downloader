# Privacy Policy - SNU eTL Video Downloader

Last updated: 2026-03-31

## Data Collection

This extension collects the following anonymous data for usage analytics via Google Analytics:

- A randomly generated anonymous ID (UUID) stored locally in your browser
- Extension events: install, update, video detection, video download
- Video title of downloaded lectures

## What We Do NOT Collect

- Personal information (name, email, address)
- Browsing history
- Cookies or authentication tokens
- eTL login credentials

## Data Usage

Collected data is used solely to understand how the extension is being used (e.g., daily download count, active users). Data is sent to Google Analytics and is not sold or transferred to any third party.

## Data Storage

- The anonymous ID is stored locally in your browser via `chrome.storage.local`
- Analytics data is processed by Google Analytics under [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
